Awarding Ceremony COECS

The College of Engineering and Computer Studies hosted its annual awarding ceremony, celebrating academic excellence and outstanding contributions in the fields of engineering and computer studies. The event recognized top-performing students for their achievements and extracurricular activities. Faculty members were also honored for their exceptional dedication and guidance.

Special awards were given for innovation and leadership, highlighting the students' impactful projects. The ceremony brought together students, faculty, and industry leaders to celebrate and inspire continued excellence. It concluded with a message of encouragement for future endeavors in their respective fields.

Acquaintance Party

The College of Engineering and Computer Studies recently held its much-anticipated acquaintance party, bringing together freshmen and senior students for a night of fun and camaraderie. The event aimed to foster connections among new and returning students through interactive games, music, and performances.

Faculty members were also present to welcome and engage with the students, creating a lively and supportive environment. The highlight of the night was a series of icebreakers and group activities that encouraged everyone to mingle and build friendships. With a relaxed and festive atmosphere, the party successfully set the tone for a collaborative and enjoyable academic year ahead.
